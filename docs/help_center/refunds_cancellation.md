# Refunds and Cancellation

## Refund Policy

### Full Refunds Eligible
- Within 14 days of initial subscription
- Within 14 days of annual renewal
- Billing errors (duplicate charges, etc.)
- Service outages exceeding SLA

### Partial Refunds
- Unused portion of annual plans (case by case)
- Downgrade mid-cycle (credit to account)
- Accidental seat additions

### Not Eligible
- After 14-day window (unless error)
- Partial months on monthly plans
- Already used services
- Repeated refund requests

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email billing@example.com
2. Include:
   - Account email
   - Charge date
   - Amount
   - Reason for refund
3. Response within 2 business days
4. Processing: 5-10 business days

## Cancellation

### Monthly Plans
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Access until current period ends

### Annual Plans
1. Same steps as monthly
2. Option to request partial refund
3. Or: Keep access until renewal date

### What Happens After

- Data retained 30 days
- Read-only access for 14 days
- Export data before deadline
- Can reactivate anytime

## Enterprise Cancellation

- Contact account manager
- 30-day notice required
- Data export assistance available
- Custom offboarding process

## Preventing Accidental Cancellation

- Confirmation required
- Email notification sent
- 24-hour "undo" window
- Billing contact notified
