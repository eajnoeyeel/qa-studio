# Working with Guest Users

## What is a Guest?

Guests are external collaborators with limited workspace access:
- Can only access specific pages shared with them
- Cannot see workspace sidebar
- Cannot create new pages (unless given permission)
- Do not count toward seat limit (up to 5 free)

## Guest vs Member

| Feature | Guest | Member |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Access scope | Specific pages | Full workspace |
| Create pages | Limited | Yes |
| See members list | No | Yes |
| Billing impact | Free (first 5) | Uses seat |
| Admin controls | No | Varies |

## Inviting Guests

1. Open the page to share
2. Click Share
3. Enter guest's email
4. Select permission (Edit/View/Comment)
5. Click Invite

Guest receives email with access link.

## Guest Limits

### Free Plan
- 5 guests total

### Team Plan
- Unlimited guests
- $5/guest/month after first 10

### Business/Enterprise
- Unlimited guests included
- Domain restrictions available

## Managing Guests

### View All Guests
Settings → Members → Guests tab

### Bulk Actions
- Remove all guests from a page
- Convert guest to member
- Export guest list

### Security

- Guests cannot be workspace admins
- Page sharing trackable in audit log
- Auto-expire guest access (Business+)

## Best Practices

- Use guests for external collaborators
- Convert to member for regular contributors
- Review guest list monthly
- Disable page sharing to prevent guest proliferation
