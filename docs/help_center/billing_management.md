# Billing and Subscription Management

## Viewing Your Plan

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. See current plan details:
   - Plan name
   - Number of seats
   - Billing cycle
   - Next payment date

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Click "Upgrade" in Billing settings
2. Select new plan
3. Choose billing cycle
4. Enter payment info (if new)
5. Confirm upgrade

**Note:** Upgrades are prorated. You only pay the difference for the remaining billing period.

## Downgrading Your Plan

1. Click "Change Plan"
2. Select lower tier
3. Review what features you'll lose
4. Confirm downgrade

**Note:** Downgrades take effect at end of current billing cycle. No prorated refunds.

## Managing Seats

### Adding Seats
1. Go to Settings → Members
2. Click "Add Members"
3. Seats added immediately
4. Prorated charge applied

### Removing Seats
1. Remove member from workspace
2. Seat count adjusts at next billing cycle
3. Cannot go below current member count

## Payment Methods

### Updating Card
1. Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Method
2. Click "Update"
3. Enter new card details
4. Save changes

### Failed Payments
- You'll receive email notification
- 3 automatic retries over 7 days
- Update payment method to retry immediately

## Invoices and Receipts

1. Go to Settings → Billing → Invoices
2. View all past invoices
3. Download PDF for each
4. Forward to accounting as needed

## Cancellation

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Provide feedback (optional)
4. Access continues until period end
5. Data retained 30 days after

## Tax Information

- Tax calculated based on billing address
- For tax exemption, contact support with documentation
- VAT IDs can be added in Billing settings
