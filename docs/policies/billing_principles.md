# Billing Principles

## Subscription Plans

| Plan | Seats | Price | Billing |
|------|-------|-------|---------|
| Free | Up to 5 | $0 | N/A |
| Pro | 1 | $10/mo | Monthly/Annual |
| Team | 5-50 | $8/seat/mo | Monthly/Annual |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Custom | Annual |

## Seat Management

### Adding Seats
- Immediate effect
- Prorated billing for current cycle
- No minimum commitment for monthly plans

### Removing Seats
- Effective end of current billing cycle
- No prorated refunds for mid-cycle removal
- Minimum 1 seat required

### Seat Calculation
- Active users count toward limit
- Guests do not count
- Deactivated users don't count after 24 hours

## Refund Policy

### Eligible for Refund
- Duplicate charges (always)
- First 14 days of new subscription
- Service outage > 24 hours (credit)
- Billing error on our part

### Not Eligible
- Mid-cycle cancellation (monthly)
- Unused portion of annual plan after 30 days
- Seat removal mid-cycle
- Feature dissatisfaction (after trial)

### Refund Process
1. Submit refund request via billing page
2. Include invoice number
3. Processing time: 5-10 business days
4. Refunds to original payment method only

## Payment Methods

Accepted:
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, MC, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Invoice billing (Enterprise, annual only)

Failed Payments:
- 3 retry attempts over 7 days
- Downgrade to free after 14 days
- 7-day grace period to restore

## Tax and Invoices

- Tax calculated based on billing address
- Invoices available in Billing Settings
- Tax exemption requires documentation
- Invoice modifications within 30 days only
