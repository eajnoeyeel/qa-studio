# Data Retention and Deletion

## Active Accounts

- All user data retained while subscription is active
- No automatic data deletion for paying customers
- Version history kept for 90 days

## After Subscription Ends

### Free Plan Downgrade
- Data preserved, read-only access after limits exceeded
- Full access restored upon upgrade
- No deletion for 6 months of inactivity

### Subscription Cancellation
- 30 days full access until period end
- 30 days read-only access after expiration
- Data deleted after 60 days total

### Account Deletion Request
- 14-day grace period (can restore)
- Permanent deletion after 14 days
- Confirmation email sent at each stage

## Enterprise Accounts

- Custom retention policies available
- Compliance holds supported
- Extended retention up to 7 years

## Data Export

- Full export available anytime
- Supported formats: JSON, Markdown, CSV
- API access for bulk export
- Export includes all workspaces user owns

## Legal Holds

- Data preserved when legal hold active
- Retention period extended indefinitely
- Customer notified unless legally prohibited

## Backup and Recovery

- Daily automated backups
- 30-day backup retention
- Recovery available within 30 days
- Contact support for recovery requests
