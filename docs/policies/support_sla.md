# Support Service Level Agreement

## Response Times

### Free Plan
- Email support only
- Response within 3 business days
- Community forums available

### Team Plan
- Email support
- Response within 24 hours
- Business hours (9am-6pm PT)

### Business Plan
- Priority email support
- Response within 4 hours
- Chat support (business hours)
- Phone callback available

### Enterprise Plan
- 24/7 support
- Response within 1 hour (critical)
- Dedicated success manager
- Slack channel option

## Priority Levels

### P1 - Critical
- Service completely unavailable
- Data loss occurring
- Security breach
- Response: 1 hour

### P2 - High
- Major feature broken
- Significant performance impact
- Affecting multiple users
- Response: 4 hours

### P3 - Medium
- Feature partially working
- Workaround available
- Single user affected
- Response: 24 hours

### P4 - Low
- Minor issue
- Enhancement request
- Documentation question
- Response: 3 business days

## Uptime SLA

### Standard
- 99.5% monthly uptime
- Excludes maintenance windows
- Status page notifications

### Enterprise
- 99.9% monthly uptime
- Custom maintenance windows
- SLA credits for violations

## Exclusions

- Issues caused by customer
- Third-party integration failures
- Force majeure events
- Scheduled maintenance

## Reporting Issues

### Best Practices
1. Check status page first
2. Search help center
3. Include:
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Expected vs actual behavior
   - Screenshots/recordings
   - Browser and OS info
